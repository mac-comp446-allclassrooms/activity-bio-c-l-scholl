Christopher Sawyer

Creator of the Tycoon Game series

Where did you go to school?

University of Strathclyde in Glasgow

Where do you work?

I started working at MemoTech and MS-DOS Games, before moving on to Tycoon Games.
I then took a break from the gaming industry before returning five years later to found 31X Ltd.
Then, in 2017, I decided to once again depart from the gaming industry, and I am still on hiatus today.

What are your accomplishments?

I have worked on nearly 30 games, including the very popular RollerCoaster Tycoon series. I created the original game as a solo machine language developer.
The RollerCoaster Tycoon IP is currently licensed to Atari, who are still releasing new games and products.
